Chocolate Cube Recipe

This chocolate cube recipe is a delightful treat for anyone who loves chocolate. Made with rich, dark chocolate and filled with a creamy ganache, these cubes are irresistible.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of dark chocolate chips
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional: nuts, berries, or sea salt for topping

Instructions:

  1. Gently melt 1 cup of chocolate chips using a double boiler or in the microwave. Be careful not to overheat.
  2. Using a small brush, coat the insides of silicone cube molds with the melted chocolate. Place in the refrigerator to set.
  3. For the ganache: Heat the heavy cream until it begins to simmer, then pour it over the remaining chocolate chips. Let sit for 1 minute.
  4. Add the butter, vanilla extract, and salt to the ganache mixture. Stir until smooth.
  5. Pour the ganache into the set chocolate cubes. Refrigerate until the ganache is set.
  6. Melt the remaining chocolate and pour it over the ganache-filled molds to seal the cubes. Refrigerate until completely set.
  7. Once set, gently remove the chocolate cubes from the molds. Optionally, top with nuts, berries, or a sprinkle of sea salt.
