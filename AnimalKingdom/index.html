Animal Kingdom

The animal kingdom constitutes an important part of the diversity of life on Earth. Thousands of species inhabit different habitats and ecosystems.

Mammals

Mammals are vertebrate animals known for their ability to produce milk and are typically found in terrestrial habitats.

Reptiles

Reptiles are cold-blooded animals covered in scales. They are usually found on land, although some also inhabit water.

Birds

Birds are warm-blooded animals known for their feathered bodies and ability to fly. They come in various shapes and sizes and are usually found in elevated habitats.
